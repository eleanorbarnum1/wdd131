Eleanor Barnum's Project Site Plan

Site Name

The site name will be "CraveWise", because the site will focus on popular food cravings, and suggesting healthier (wiser) alternatives.

Scenarios

Questions might include "What are some healthier alternatives to potato chips?" or "How should I start my healthy eating journey?"

Color Scheme

I really like this bright green color(#4CAF50), and I think it fits well with the peppy, bright and fun vibe I'm going for. I plan to use it as the background color, along with a bit of a white gradient.

As for the buttons and extra little splashes of color, I want to use a range of greens (#CDDC39,#388E3C), as well as an energetic orange(#FF9800) to make it pop.

And then of course, white and black for the text/ final little accents.

Typography

As shown in the document, there are two fonts I plan to use. The first is "Slacky", and will be used for headings, titles, and buttons. The other is "Pathway Extreme" and will be the basic paragraph font.
